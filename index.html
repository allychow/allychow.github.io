I'm currently in Waterloo, ON in my 2A term, but soon I'll be interning at Microsoft in Redmond! I have a soft spot in my heart for program management and design, but writing code is my current love in life.

As the current Technology Coordinator for UW Women in STEM , I teach workshops on web design, mentor programmers, and help run exciting events. I'm also a mediocre player of the saxophone, badminton, and if you check out my Spotify, you'll find that I listen to one song on repeat for days at a time.
